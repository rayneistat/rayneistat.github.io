InstantEye Arduino Datalogger

June 2021 - December 2021

I made 2 simple measuring instruments to support vehicle development: A temperature logger with 8 thermocouples and an accelerometer logger with 2 accelerometers. For each project, I collected data from each sensor and wrote it onto an SD card for analysis.

The pictures included here are of the accelerometer setup, and its corresponding data. I plotted a time series as well as an FFT of vibration data.
