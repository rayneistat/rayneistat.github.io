InstantEye Vehicle Packaging: Component Re-design

March 2021 - April 2021

Our manufacturing team discovered that some parts were getting damaged during delivery. I was tasked with identifying the root cause of this breakage, as well as redesigning the part, testing it, and deploying it as an official change. During development, I performed several drop tests to determine the magnitude of the problem, and developed a molding process using a 2-part, 5-minute silicone kit. The result was an improved part that did not break during transit.
